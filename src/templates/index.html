How it works

Real road data is fetched from OpenStreetMap for the area between your points. A graph is built where intersections are nodes and street segments are weighted edges.

The selected algorithm runs on this real road graph — gray lines show the road network, yellow shows edges explored, green shows the final path.

Place points 1–5 km apart for best results.
